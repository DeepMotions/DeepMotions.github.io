About Us

In 2015, Sven founded DeepMotions.
The idea of DeepMotions is to discover unknown, young artists, who want to share their music with the world. DeepMotions strives to help these producers by giving them a platform to do this on.

We strive to create the best music selection around the globe. Also to help all of you to get through your day.

In 2016, Sven came across Hidde through a design query and decided to bring him on board of DeepMotions. Hidde & Sven had a fine partnership and DeepMotions grew even bigger than they could have ever imagined. They hope it will grow even further.
